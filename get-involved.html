How to get involved...


Donations

We appreciate any monetary donation. By providing monetary donations you provide critical funds to help cover the ongoing costs and providing these services for free. Your money is going towards essential items that is perishable, storage space for the food and packaging. Monetary donations can either be once-off or regular donations.

As a not-for profit organisation you are also eligible to claim 1/3 of your donations back at the end of the financial year.
