About Us


Turning Point Trust is a not-for-profit organisation in New Zealand helping Kiwis in need. We provide tailor-made food boxes that includes breakfast, dinner and snacks to last at least a week. You can either donate food to our charity, donate a box or through monetary donations. We then pack the food boxes based on the family's needs.


Why donate to our charity?

Every day thousands of families are struggling across the Waikato and we want to help by providing immediate relief through food boxes. However, unlike other charities we donâ€™t want to just put a band-aid on the problem. We want to identify the root causes of why a family needed a food box in the first place. This way we deliver long term solutions through budgeting advice, help getting employment, getting a driver's licence or anything else a family might need to turn their lives around.

With Turning Point Trust, you can be assured that your donations will go to people in your community that needs it the most. We believe in giving people a hand up rather than a hand out.
