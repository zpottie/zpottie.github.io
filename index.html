What is Turning Point?

Turning Point Trust is a not-for-profit organisation in New Zealand helping Kiwis in need. We provide tailor-made food boxes that includes breakfast, dinner and snacks to last at least a week. However, unlike other charities we don’t want to just put a band-aid on the problem. We want to identify the root causes of why a family needed a food box in the first place.
